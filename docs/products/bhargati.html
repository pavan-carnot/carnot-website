What is BharGati?

BharGati AI is an AI-powered system for movement analysis and performance intelligence. It captures, interprets, and quantifies human motion using computer vision, deep learning, and biomechanical modeling.

It improves athletic assessment, rehabilitation tracking, and real-time movement interpretation by providing accurate metrics and useful insights directly from visual data inputs. There is no need for wearable sensors or external tracking systems.

Whether you’re a sports scientist, coach, physical therapist, or research professional, BharGati is designed to turn movement into intelligence.

Biomechanics analytics
Real-time feedback
No wearables needed

The Problem BharGati Solves

Traditional motion analysis systems are often:

  • Expensive and requires a lot of hardware.
  • Restricted to lab environments.
  • Dependent on specialized sensors or markers.
  • Difficult to use in real-world training and field conditions.

This limits access to high-quality biomechanical insights for many practitioners, athletes, and clinics.

BharGati brings precise assessment out of the lab and into practice.

BharGati addresses this by offering camera-based AI motion analysis that is affordable, scalable, and usable in real training or clinical settings.
